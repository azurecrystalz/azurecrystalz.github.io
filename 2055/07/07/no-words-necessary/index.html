No Words Necessary
言葉はなくとも
Start Reading
Kaede informs Kuguri and Raito of a gym in the HAMA House, and Daniel witnesses the hijinks that ensue...
Location
HAMA House
Chapters
2
Characters
Kuguri, Raito, Daniel
Translator
aurora
Proofreaders
dimi (JP) & shay (ENG)

Track 1

Location: HAMA House - Dorms

Welcome, Chief, to our “Folly Jolly Paradise”1.

All of our furniture has been set up, so feel free to take a look around.

Woah, you even bought a chair for Kinari-kun!

As for how it fits in with the room’s decor… well it’s definitely not the most cohesive, but…

It’s quite nice, isn’t it, cicám?

Wouldn’t this chair be a nice fit for you too?

Nooo, it’d fit Kinari-kun best, since it’s his.

Phone alarm goes off.

Oh… it’s time for my workout.

Your workout?

There’s a gym down in the basement.

Muneuji-kun and I have been exercising there lately.

Oh? Well, it’s good to work out.

I like pushing my muscles to their limits too.

Is that so?

In that case, would you like to go together?

……

Haha, he’s ignoring me.

Aha… ha…

Well, anyone can use it at any time, so feel free to check it out whenever you want!

I’ll be going to my workout now!

……

Location: HAMA House - Gym

(Hmm… there’s quite the array of special equipment.)

Oh, Kuguri. You’re here too?

…(Sigh).

How about it? Since we’re both here, why don’t we workout togeth–

Kuguri quickly moves away.

Ignored again… Well, fine.

I won’t fall behind either.

Track 2

Location: HAMA House - Gym

Sound of machines clanging.

(...Lets out a breath.)

More machines clanging.

(Huff…)

Kuguri's machine clangs.

(Grunt…!)

Raito's machine clangs.

(Groan…!)

Oh, you two are the new guys from the evening group. Looks like you’re really goin’ hard—

Loud machine banging.

(Not a peep out of either of ‘em, huh…)

(I s‘pose that means there's a lil contest of strength going down.)

(Pant…)

(Is he gonna hop on the treadmill next?)

(I’ll just stick to watching them for now.)

…

(Raito’s going for a run too… They’ve got a pretty similar routine, so maybe they do get along well.)

Sound of treadmills starting, whirring to a fast setting, and running footsteps

(Pant… pant…)

(Huff huff…)

(Woah woah, easy there! They’re almost at max speed!)

(Don’t go breaking these bad boys!)

(Heavy breathing…)

(Pant…)

(Looks like Raito won… or no, it almost tied. What a good match.)

(So next they’re gonna… do some bench presses. This is probably where we’ll have a winner.)

Machines banging.

(Grunts…!)

(Huff… pant…)

More machines banging.

(A narrow victory for Kuguri?!)

(Looks like the leg press is up ne—)

(Exasperated sigh). What a joke.

I think I’ll go take a shower.

(Oh, Kuguri’s backing out of the fight?)

That was a good workout…

(Huffs)...

(Seems like they settled whatever was between them?!)

(Even if they don’t talk much, they communicate with their muscles… Maybe it’s some sort of strong bond…)

Translation Notes:

(1): Ukiuki usually means exciting/happy and this gives the same vibe as something named Lovey Dovey or Mopey Wopey
